Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Pitambar Vastra ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you use this website, and how it's used.

What we collect

When you optionally fill in the "Delivery Details" section at checkout, we collect the name, phone number, and address you provide. This is used only to deliver your order and to contact you about it.

If you choose "Remember my details for next time," this information is saved only in your own browser's local storage on your device — not on our servers or any database. You can clear it anytime using the "Clear" button in the cart.

Your cart and wishlist contents are also stored only in your own browser, the same way.

Payments

Payments are processed directly by Razorpay, a licensed payment gateway. We do not see or store your card, UPI, or banking details — that information is handled entirely by Razorpay under their own security and privacy standards.

Order communication

After a successful payment, you'll be prompted to send your order and delivery details to us via WhatsApp, so we can arrange delivery. This conversation happens on WhatsApp directly between you and us.

No accounts, no tracking sign-in

This website does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage (not cookies) to remember your cart, wishlist, and optional saved delivery details. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us unless you complete a checkout.
